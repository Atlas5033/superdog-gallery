About Superdog Smidge

Superdog Smidge is a daily comic experiment starring a heroic little dachshund who explores a new emotion or mood each day. From joy to frustration, awe to anxiety, Smidge reflects the emotional rollercoaster of life — all in four expressive panels.

This project is a mix of passion and play: AI-generated comic art, mood-driven storytelling prompts, and a bit of tech wizardry stitched together by an amateur developer with a love for comics, code, and canines.

Smidge's adventures are here to make you smile, think, or just enjoy a fresh daily dose of dachshund-powered emotional insight. Whether you’re here for a laugh or a little relatability, we’re glad you found us.
